## Design Context

### Users
A broad professional audience, including potential clients and the creative community, looking for a high-quality and technically sophisticated showcase of work.

### Brand Personality
**Precise, Clean, Professional.** 

### Aesthetic Direction
**Mechanical Minimalism.** The interface should feel like a high-precision instrument—stable, smooth, and meticulously crafted. It prioritizes clarity and intentional motion over decorative fluff.

### Design Principles
1. **Mechanical Precision**: Use exponential easing and smooth GSAP/Framer Motion interactions to make the UI feel mechanical and "engineered."
2. **Typographic Clarity**: Rely on a strong modular type scale and ample whitespace to maintain a clean, "Swiss-style" editorial hierarchy.
3. **Purposeful Depth**: Utilize advanced CSS (glassmorphism, blend modes) sparingly to create depth and focus, ensuring they never detract from readability.
4. **Optimistic Seamlessness**: Page transitions and navigation should be instantaneous and fluid, reinforcing the brand's commitment to quality.
